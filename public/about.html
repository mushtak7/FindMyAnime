About FindMyAnime

Your go-to platform for discovering, tracking, and discussing anime and manga.

🎯 Our Mission

FindMyAnime was built with one goal: to make discovering your next favorite anime or manga as easy and enjoyable as possible. We combine powerful search tools, community discussions, and personalized tracking to create the ultimate otaku hub.

✨ Features

🔍Smart Search & Filters
📚Watchlist & Library
💬Community Discussions
📅Airing Schedule
✨"Find Me Something" Quiz
📰Anime & Manga News

🛠️ Built With

FindMyAnime is powered by Jikan API (unofficial MyAnimeList API), Node.js, Express, and PostgreSQL. The frontend is built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for maximum performance.
